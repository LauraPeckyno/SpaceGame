Game Instructions

Are you ready to fish? Your goal is to catch 6 fish for dinner.

To play, click the "Cast & Reel" button to cast your line. If you get a bite, you'll have to reel the fish in by clicking the "Cast & Reel" button again.

If the fish swims away, you'll lose some stamina. If you run out of stamina before you catch enough fish, the game is over.

You can retreat from the game at any time by clicking the "Give Up" button.

You can reset your game with the "Reset" button.
